Scrambled Eggs

One of the easiest and tastiest go-to recipe that takes just three ingredients to make. It just takes you 10-20 minutes to make it.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbs Butter
  • 1 tbs Cream

Method:

  1. Melt butter in frying pan on a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter is melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Uing a sapatula fold the eggs from the edge of
    the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you
    are making an omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
    (it will continue to cook on the plate
    until served)
